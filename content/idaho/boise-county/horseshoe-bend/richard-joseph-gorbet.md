## Case Details
Gorbet was last seen in Horseshoe Bend, Idaho at 10:0 p.m. on May 25, 2018. He became "despondent" after an argument with a relative, left on foot and has never been heard from again. Although Gorbet is not mentally disabled, he is considered a vulnerable adult. He hadn't left home in about ten years prior to his disappearance, didn't have a driver's license and was dependent on his family and friends. His case remains unsolved.
