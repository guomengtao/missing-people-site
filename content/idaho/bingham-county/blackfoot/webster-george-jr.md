## Case Details
George was last seen in Blackfoot, Idaho on September 1, 1982. He has never been heard from again. Prior to his disappearance, he had a history of trouble with the law, including convictions for burglary, assault and driving while intoxicated. George has since been declared legally dead. Few details are available in his case.
