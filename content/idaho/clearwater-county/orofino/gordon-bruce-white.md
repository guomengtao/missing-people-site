## Case Details
White was last seen riding his ATV on his property in the 7000 block of Dent Bride Road in Orofino, Idaho at 4:00 p.m. on June 10, 2020. He had left home without his cellular phone or wallet, and has never been heard from again. His ATV was found abandoned on his property the next morning, intact and with gas in the tank, but there was no sign of White and an extensive search turned up no indication of his whereabouts. Few details are available in his case.
