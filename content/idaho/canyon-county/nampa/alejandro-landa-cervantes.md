## Case Details
Cervantes was last seen in Nampa, Idaho on June 10, 2008. He has never been heard from again. He is from Washington and had moved to Idaho with a friend to find work the same year he disappeared. In 2010, the friend Cervantes moved to Idaho with was shot to death during an alleged drug deal. It's not clear if the friend is a suspect in Cervantes's case or if Cervantes's disappearance was drug-related. Few details are available in his case.
