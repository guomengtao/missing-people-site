## Case Details
Mandigo was last seen at his residence in Oldtown, Idaho on June 17, 2020. His home burned to the ground. His car was left parked out front, and Mandigo was never seen or heard from again after the fire, so he was presumed to have perished. However, authorities couldn't find any sign of Mandigo's body in the ashes of his home. Few details are available in his case.
