## Case Details
Lockhart was last seen at a Kroger grocery store in Moundsville, West Virginia on March 28, 2015. She had a phone conversation with someone on April 2, and this is the last time anyone heard from her. She lived in Friendly, West Virginia at the time of her disappearance, and she has ties to Weirton, West Virginia and Steubenville, Ohio. She may have traveled to Florida or to Toronto, Ontario after her disappearance. Few details are available in her case.
