# Earl Kuhl

![Earl Kuhl](https://img.gudq.com/missing/west-virginia/harrison-county/clarksburg/earl-kuhl/earl-kuhl-1.webp)

## Case Details
Kuhl was last seen in Clarksburg, West Virginia on April 8, 1998. He was a patient Louis A. Johnson Veterans Affairs Medical Center on Medical Drive at the time of his disappearance; he was admitted for a heart-related procedure. A nurse noticed him missing shortly before 1:00 a.m. Kuhl has never been heard from again. Authorities believe he may have drowned in the West Fork River, but searched of the river turned up no evidence. Few details are available in his case. He resided in Burnsville, West Virginia in 1998.
