## Case Details
Pugh was last seen in Charleston, West Virginia on April 17, 2018. A witness reported seeing her get into a red truck outside the Go-Mart on the west side of the city. She has never been heard from again. Her family reported her missing on April 22. Pugh has been known to frequent the west side of Charleston, particularly Washington Street and Lee Street.
