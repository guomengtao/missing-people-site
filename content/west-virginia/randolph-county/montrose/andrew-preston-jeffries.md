## Case Details
Jeffries was last seen on the left fork of Cherry Fork Road in Montrose, West Virginia on August 20, 2018. He left his cellular phone, clothes, wallet, money, and identification behind at home and has never been heard from again. His stepfather's motorcycle, which Jeffries had been riding that day, was found abandoned on Hunters Fork Road in Barbour County, West Virginia. Few details are available in his case.
