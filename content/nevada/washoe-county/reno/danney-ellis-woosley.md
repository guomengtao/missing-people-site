# Danney Ellis Woosley

## Case Details
Woosley was last seen Reno, Nevada sometime in 1984 or 1985. He went to North Hollywood, California with a friend, riding a Yamaha off-road motorcycle. He has never been heard from again and was reported missing on August 25, 1985. When Woosley's father died in 2011 and his stepmother died in 2019, Woosley was listed in both obituaries as having survived them. Few details are available in his case.
