# Rick Lewis

## Case Details
Lewis was last seen at his home on Geary Street in Reno, Nevada on August 3, 2022. Prior to his disappearance he had said multiple times that he wanted to "go into the mountains where no one will find him" and several firearms were discovered missing from the residence after his disappearance. He didn't take his identification, debit card, clothing or other personal items, however. Lewis may have been a suicide victim, but his remains have never been found. He has been known to hike the Halo Trail and the Keystone Canyon Trail on Peavine.
