# Mary Elizabeth Carter

## Case Details
Carter was last seen in Reno, Nevada on December 14, 1984. She planned to go to Carson City, Nevada to find a job, but it is unknown if she ever arrived. Some time after her disappearance, Carter's wallet was found and turned into Harvey's, an establishment in Lake Tahoe, California. Her case remains unsolved. Some agencies state that Carter went missing on December 3, 1984.
