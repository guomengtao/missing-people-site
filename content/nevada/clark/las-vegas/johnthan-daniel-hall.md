## Case Details
Hall was last seen in Las Vegas, Nevada on February 13, 2019. He had moved from Mississippi to Las Vegas in 2012 and worked two jobs, one assisting his father with maintenance work at a trailer park and the other at the Las Vegas Cue Club. He went to his Cue Club job that evening and coworkers saw him there, but no one remembered seeing him leave. He has never been heard from again. Hall last spoke to his mother several days prior to his disappearance. They were on the phone for nearly an hour, and his mother sensed something was wrong, but he wouldn't tell her anything. He left his bicycle and moped behind at his home. His case remains unsolved.
