## Case Details
Schock was last seen on January 31, 1982. He lived in Olathe, Kansas but was away from home on a business trip. He took a side trip to Las Vegas, Nevada during his business trip; this is his last known location. He never returned home and his family never heard from him again. His family in North Dakota reported him missing to the Ward County Sheriff's Department in North Dakota in mid-February; that agency is investigating his case. Few details are available in Schock's disappearance. He may be driving a tan and White 1981 Chevrolet Corvette.
