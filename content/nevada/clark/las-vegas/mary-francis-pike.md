## Case Details
Pike traveled with her boyfriend from her home state of Kentucky to the Las Vegas, Nevada area in 1993 or 1994. Her family last heard from her by telephone sometime in April 1994; they believe she was in Las Vegas at the time. Some time later, Pike's boyfriend returned to Kentucky without her. Pike's family reported her missing on July 9. Little information is available in her case. Nevada police are investigating.
