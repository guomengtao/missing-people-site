## Case Details
Pappas was last seen in the vicinity of Robindale and Haven Street in Las Vegas, Nevada on the evening of August 18, 1976. He left home driving his White two-door 1973 Ford Gran Torino with Nevada license plates, saying he was going to meet someone at a local restaurant about selling his boat. He never returned home and has never been heard from again. Few details are available in his case.
