## Case Details
Huggins was last seen in Las Vegas, Nevada on August 29, 1994. She has never been heard from again. She worked as a sex worker in 1994, and lived in the Golden City Motel in the 1400 block of south Las Vegas Boulevard. She did not own a vehicle. Huggins may have traveled to California after her disappearance. She vanished under suspicious circumstances. Few details are available in her case.
