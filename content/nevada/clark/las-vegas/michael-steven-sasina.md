# Michael Steven Sasina

## Case Details
Sasina was last seen in Las Vegas, Nevada on December 22, 1986. He left home at about 9:00 a.m. to walk to the 7-Eleven store at Russell and Mountain Vista; he would walk to the store for exercise. He didn't intend to be gone for long and didn't take much money or any extra clothing with him, and left a load of laundry in the washing machine to finish when he got back. He never returned and has never been heard from again.
