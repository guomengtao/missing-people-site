## Case Details
Schell was last seen in the 2700 block of Eldora Avenue in Las Vegas, Nevada on December 22, 1986. She called her out-of-state family and told them she had no money. After that, she never spoke to them again and all her family's calls to her went unanswered. Her vehicle was subsequently found abandoned in Los Angeles, California. Few details are available in Schell's case. She is missing under unknown circumstances.
