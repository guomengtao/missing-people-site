## Case Details
Veach was last seen at a movie theater in Las Vegas on November 10, 2014. He called his ex-girlfriend to say he was going camping overnight. He planned to visit a hidden cave he'd found on a previous hike, one shaped like a capital M. He has never been heard from again and his vehicle was recovered at a recreation area north of Las Vegas, near Nellis Air Force base. His cellular phone was found up a canyon, near a deep vertical mine shaft about a four-hour hike from his car. His case remains unsolved.
