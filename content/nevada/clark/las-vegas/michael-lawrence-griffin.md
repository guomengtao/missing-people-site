# Michael Lawrence Griffin

![Michael Lawrence Griffin](https://img.gudq.com/missing/nevada/clark/las-vegas/michael-lawrence-griffin/michael-lawrence-griffin-1.webp)

## Case Details
Griffin was last seen in Las Vegas, Nevada on March 1, 1958. He and his girlfriend, Donnis Redman, were in San Pedro, California when they eloped to Las Vegas, a four-and-a-half-hour drive away. Neither of them have ever been heard from again. Griffin's car, a White 1950 or 1951 Dodge Clipper with twin exhaust and loud mufflers, was found in Williams, Arizona several days later. Williams is more than a three-hour drive from Las Vegas. Los Angeles, California police are investigating Donnis and Griffin's cases.
