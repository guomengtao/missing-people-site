## Case Details
Miller was last seen in Las Vegas, Nevada on February 28, 1989, at his business in the 3900 block of West Dewey Drive. His employees said he was going to a location on Blue Diamond Road to get some money from someone, then planned to go to the yard and deliver storage containers to job sites. But when his son went to the location on Blue Diamond Road, he found out Miller had never been there. He has never been heard from again. Few details are available in his case.
