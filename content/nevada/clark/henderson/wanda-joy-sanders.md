## Case Details
Sanders was last seen in Henderson, Nevada on February 11, 1985. She had rented an apartment, and was last seen when she left her mother's home on Pueblo Boulevard at 8:00 a.m. to sign rental documents and have the power turned on. Sanders didn't own a car and left on foot, carrying $60 in cash. Her appointment with the apartment manager was at at 11:00 but she never arrived and has never been heard from again. Her mother reported her missing on February 13. Sanders left behind a young daughter. Her case remains unsolved.
