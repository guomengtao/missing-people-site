## Case Details
Condos was last seen in Mesquite, Nevada on August 10, 2020. He arrived there after leaving Teller County, Colorado. He has never been heard from again. He may be driving a blue and White 1993 Ford Econoline E350 van with the Colorado license plate number BEHG14. It has room for 15 passengers, used to belong to a church and has the words "Journey Quest.org" in faint lettering on the side. A photo of the van is posted with this case summary. Few details are available in Condos's case. Both Nevada and Colorado police are investigating.
