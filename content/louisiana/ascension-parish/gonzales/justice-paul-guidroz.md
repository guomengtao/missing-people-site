## Case Details
Guidroz was last seen in Gonzales, Louisiana on December 22, 2005. He got into a minor traffic accident after leaving home and fled the scene on foot, presumably because he had bench warrants out for his arrest. He was last seen walking in the vicinity of Holton Road and Norward Road. He has never been heard from again. Guidroz was heavily involved in drugs and the local drug culture at the time of his disappearance. Authorities believe he may have been the victim of a homicide. His case remains unsolved.
