## Case Details
Duplush had dialysis treatment at a medical facility on Burnside Avenue in Gonzales, Louisiana, and was last heard from at 11:30 p.m. that same day. He has never been heard from. He was reported missing the next afternoon. At 9:00 p.m. that same day, his gold 2004 Toyota Solara was found abandoned in a rural area. A photo of the vehicle is posted with this case summary. It was parked on the side of Panama Road close to Highway 22, unlocked, with keys and Duplush's medication inside. Duplush's disappearance remains unsolved.
