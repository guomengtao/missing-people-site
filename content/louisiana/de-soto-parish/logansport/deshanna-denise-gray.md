## Case Details
Gray was last seen in the Japan Quarters area of Logansport, Louisiana on November 22, 2000, the day prior to Thanksgiving. She was walking on Crawford Street when she got into a small blue pickup truck at approximately 11:00 a.m. The truck may have had Texas or Arkansas license plates, the and the driver was a White male. Gray has never been heard from again. Gray's mother stated she had never dropped out of sight before. She left behind two children. Her case remains unsolved.
