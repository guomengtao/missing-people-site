## Case Details
Russell moved from Falkville, Alabama to New Orleans, Louisiana in July 2021. He was homeless by choice and established an address at an abandoned auto shop, where he received mail. His family last heard from him on August 20, 2021, and he told them he was no longer living at the auto shop, but he didn't indicate he was distressed. He has never been heard from again. It's uncharacteristic of Russell to be out of touch with his family. He has lived in Illinois and Georgia in the past. Few details are available in his case.
