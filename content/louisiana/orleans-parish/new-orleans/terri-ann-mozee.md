## Case Details
Mozee was last seen getting into a taxi in New Orleans, Louisiana on March 3, 2001. She had some luggage with her and said she was going on a trip but would be back that weekend. She has never been heard from again. Mozee hasn't collected any of her Social Security benefits since her disappearance. She was declared legally dead in 2004. Her case remains unsolved.
