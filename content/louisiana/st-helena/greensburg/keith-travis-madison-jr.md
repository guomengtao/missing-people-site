## Case Details
Madison was last seen walking down Hall Lindsey Road in Greensburg, Louisiana on February 20, 2021. He left home with his gun and wallet, but without his chewing tobacco, without saying where he was going, and has never been heard from again. Prior to his disappearance, Madison had confided to his daughter about some domestic problems: his stepdaughter and her boyfriend lived with him and his wife, and Madison didn't get along with his stepdaughter's boyfriend and wanted him to leave. It's not clear whether this had anything to do with his disappearance. An extensive search of the area turned up no indication of Madison's whereabouts. His case remains unsolved.
