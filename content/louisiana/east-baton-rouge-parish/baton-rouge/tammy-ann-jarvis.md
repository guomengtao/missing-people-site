## Case Details
Jarvis was living with an aunt in Baton Rouge, Louisiana in May 1989. She disappeared while her aunt was out of town. Her neighbors stated they last saw Jarvis on May 10, being dragged into the backyard by her boyfriend, who was armed with a shotgun. She has never been heard from again. Jarvis's boyfriend has never been charged in her disappearance. He was convicted of second-degree murder in 1992 in the shooting death of another girlfriend, and has been incarcerated.
