## Case Details
O'Banion was last seen leaving her waitressing shift at Southern Comfort on Airline Highway in Baton Rouge, Louisiana at 2:00 a.m. on October 23, 1985. She never arrived back at her residence in the 5900 block of Stumberg Lane that day, and her mother reported her missing. O'Banion has never been heard from again. Few details are available in her case.
