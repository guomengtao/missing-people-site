## Case Details
Jarreau was last seen leaving his residence in Baton Rouge, Louisiana on October 23, 1991. He told his wife of less than a year that he was going to the store. He had only a few dollars on him when he left. Jarreau's truck was later found in the parking lot of a Waffle House restaurant in Baton Rouge. He has never been heard from again. He was employed as a construction worker in 1991. His case remains unsolved.
