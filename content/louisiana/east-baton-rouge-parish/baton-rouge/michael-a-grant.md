## Case Details
Grant was last seen in Baton Rouge, Louisiana sometime during mid-June, 1991. He borrowed ten dollars from his mother and vanished afterward. Two years after his disappearance, Grant's mother got several telephone calls from people who claimed to have seen her son. The last caller said he'd seen him walking down Madison Avenue in Baton Rouge. None of the sightings have ever been confirmed. Grant's case remains unsolved.
