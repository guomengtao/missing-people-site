## Case Details
Davis was last seen when she dropped off her two children at her mother's home in the Glen Oaks area of Baton Rouge, Louisiana at 4:45 p.m. on December 27, 2018, her 35th birthday. She has never been heard from again. Her vehicle, a White 2015 Dodge Challenger, was later found abandoned in the 6900 block of Scenic Highway near 72nd Avenue in Baton Rouge, with her purse, keys and cash inside. Authorities stated there was no evidence of foul play in Davis's case, but it's uncharacteristic of her to leave without warning and her family fears she may have met with foul play. Her case remains unsolved. Baker, Louisiana police are investigating.
