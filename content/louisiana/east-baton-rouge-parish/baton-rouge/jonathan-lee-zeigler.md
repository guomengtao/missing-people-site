## Case Details
Zeigler was last seen leaving his grandmother's house in Baton Rouge, Louisiana on the morning of March 10, 1992. He lived in Denman Springs, Louisiana, but had spent the night at his grandmother's home. Zeigler has never been heard from again. Authorities believe he may have been taken against his will. Some suspicious vehicles were seen near his grandmother's home at about the time he vanished. Texas authorities are assisting with Zeigler's investigation. He may be in Florida. His case remains unsolved and foul play is suspected.
