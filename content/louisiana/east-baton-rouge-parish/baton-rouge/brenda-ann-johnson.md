## Case Details
Johnson was last seen in Baton Rouge, Louisiana on January 3, 2014. She has never been heard from again. Her last known address was on south River Road in Addis, Louisiana, and after her disappearance she may have been seen on Sherwood Forest Boulevard in Baton Rouge or on Highway 415 by the Motel 6 in Port Allen, Louisiana. Few details are available in her case.
