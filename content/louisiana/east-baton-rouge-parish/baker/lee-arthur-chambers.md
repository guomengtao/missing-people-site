## Case Details
Chambers was last seen when he was dropped off at his residence in the 5400 block of Myrtle Street in Baker, Louisiana on December 11, 2017. He has never been heard from again. Due to his progressing dementia, his family was in the process of having him moved to a retirement home in Zachary, Louisiana at the time of his disappearance. An extensive search turned up no indication of Chambers's whereabouts. Few details are available in his case.
