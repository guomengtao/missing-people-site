# Nancy Hawk

![Nancy Hawk](https://img.gudq.com/missing/louisiana/jefferson-parish/metairie/nancy-hawk/nancy-hawk-1.webp)

## Case Details
Hawk was last seen in the 1100 block of Clearview Boulevard in Metairie, Louisiana at 8:00 p.m. on August 12, 2011. She left after getting into an argument with the man she lived with. Her roommate stated she got into a green Honda SUV in front of their residence and this was the last time he saw her. Hawk owns a flea market in New Orleans, Louisiana. She never showed up for work the next day and has never been heard from again. A relative reported her missing; Hawk's roommate said he didn't notify police because she'd left home in the past. Foul play is suspected in her case, which remain unsolved.
