## Case Details
Guillory was last seen in Metairie, Louisiana on February 16, 1999 at approximately 12:00 p.m. She was at her apartment in the 4000 block of Saxton Street at the time. Guillory disappeared under unclear circumstances and has never been seen again. She has a history of dropping out of sight for extended time periods. Guillory was born in Japan and adopted at the age of five. She used to live in Canada and may have returned to that country, but little information is available in her case and it remains unsolved.
