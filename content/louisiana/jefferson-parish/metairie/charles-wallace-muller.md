## Case Details
Muller was last seen in the 200 block Helios Avenue in Metairie, Louisiana on March 14, 1977. He has never been heard from again. His car, a light green 1964 Volkswagen Beetle, was found two weeks later, parked at a bridge maintenance parking area under the east bank of the Huey P. Long Bridge. Few details are available in his case.
