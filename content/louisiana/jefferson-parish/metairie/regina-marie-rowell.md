## Case Details
Rowell was last seen in the 6900 block of Veterans Boulevard in Metairie, Louisiana on March 10, 1981. She lived in Metairie and worked at the Veterans Administration Medical Center at the time of her disappearance. She has never been heard from again. On March 16, her vehicle was found abandoned on the Huey P. Long Bridge with her purse, wallet and identification inside it. Few details are available in her case.
