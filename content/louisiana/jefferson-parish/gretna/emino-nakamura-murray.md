## Case Details
Murray was last seen leaving her workplace in the 100 block of Kass Street in Gretna, Louisiana on August 20, 1982. She was driving a blue four-door 1973 Ford with the Louisiana license plate number 147L267. Authorities believe she was en route to her boyfriend's residence.
