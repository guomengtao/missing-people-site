## Case Details
Martin was last seen in the 300 block of Ormond Meadows in Destrehan, Louisiana on February 11, 2020. He got into an argument with his girlfriend, left on foot at 8:00 p.m. and has never been heard from again. Prior to his disappearance, Martin had made statements implying he planned to harm himself. Few details are available in his case.
