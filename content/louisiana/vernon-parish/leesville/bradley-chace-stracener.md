## Case Details
Stracener was last seen in Leesville, Louisiana on October 30, 2019. He left home to return a car to his girlfriend, who lived ten or twelve miles away, but he never arrived there. At 6:30 a.m. on October 31, the car was found abandoned in front of a steel gate on Bundick's Road in the Sandy Hill area, about two miles from where Stracener was last seen. It was unlocked, the keys were in the passenger seat, and there were no indications of a struggle. Stracener has been known to spend months at a time living in the woods. Few details are available in his case.
