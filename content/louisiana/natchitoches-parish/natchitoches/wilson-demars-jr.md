## Case Details
Demars was last seen driving his White 1981 Dodge pickup truck in Natchitoches, Louisiana on June 13, 1988. He is from Houston, Texas and disappeared while visiting a relative in Louisiana for the weekend. He has never been heard from again. Few details are available in his case, but foul play is suspected.
