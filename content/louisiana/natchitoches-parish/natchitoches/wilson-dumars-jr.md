## Case Details
Dumars was last seen in Natchitoches, Louisiana at 2:00 p.m. on June 13, 1988, driving his White 1981 Dodge pickup truck. He lived in Houston, Texas and was visiting a relative for the weekend at the time of his disappearance. He has never been heard from again. Few details are available in his case.
