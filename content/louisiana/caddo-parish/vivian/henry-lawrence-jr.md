## Case Details
Lawrence was last seen in Vivian, Louisiana on November 27, 1998. He was in the 300 block of Choctaw Street at the time of his disappearance. Lawrence planned to travel to Dallas, Texas with a friend. He never returned to Louisiana and has not been heard from again. Few details are available in his case.
