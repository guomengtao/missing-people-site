## Case Details
O'Con was last seen in Shreveport, Louisiana on June 9, 1981. She had been admitted to the veteran's hospital suffering from extreme stress and exhaustion. Every evening, her mother and aunt visited her in the lobby. On the day of her disappearance, O'Con went downstairs with her milk and was last seen by a security guard on the hospital grounds. She has never been heard from again. Few details are available in her case.
